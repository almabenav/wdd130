Overview

Purpose

Are you starting your own business? Or are you just interested in finding out about the different types of entrepreneurship in your city in a simple way? Many times we don't support local talent. With Go Entrepreneur you will reach the next level. The user will be able to access everything in one place, full of easy tools on the page not only for clients but also for the entrepreneurs, where they can get in contact with people interested in helping their business. And much more information to create an online community. An excellent opportunity to support and be supported.

Audience

For Entrepreneurs, of all ages, people who want to get into the business world and start their own business, from beginners to small business owners who want to make themselves known. Also people who like to consume local businesses, who find it difficult to know what their city has to offer. And anyone who wants to learn and support this community, either by consuming or investing in the businesses. The site will have a minimalist and simple look that will give people an air of comfort and confidence.
