Entrepreneurship in these days means a lot to the economy to keep it dynamic, alive and proactive. The culture of entrepreneurship helps business ideas to materialize, expand and become sustainable solutions providing society with new products and services. Entrepreneurship also promotes innovation, productivity and employment by creating opportunities.

ENTREPRENEURSHIP TODAY

1.

Currently, entrepreneurship represents the need to produce and generate with their own resources, either starting with small businesses or the simple fact of going from being employees to become employers, thus being entrepreneurship something very much seen in these times and also very much imposed by previous generations.

2.

Entrepreneurship is presented as an option and a good opportunity for people to contribute and develop their own ideas and that these are aimed at improving the quality of life in the society in which it seeks to make the venture.

3.

Little by little it has gained the importance it has always deserved because it covers the needs of many people at the moment of looking for and finding an economic independence.

We conclude with the positive aspects of entrepreneurship which would be, independence, autonomy, effort, work and perseverance.
